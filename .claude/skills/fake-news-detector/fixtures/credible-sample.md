# Evergreen County Publishes Annual Drinking Water Report

Evergreen County's Department of Public Works released its annual drinking water report on Tuesday, saying routine tests found contaminant levels within state and federal limits. The report says samples were collected from 34 monitoring sites between January and March and reviewed by the state environmental laboratory.

County health director Maria Chen said in the report that two wells will receive additional maintenance this summer because mineral levels increased, though the levels remained below the regulatory threshold. The department also posted the full data table, sampling dates, and contact information for residents who want to ask questions about individual results.

The state environmental agency confirmed in a separate notice that it reviewed the county's submission and did not issue a violation. The notice said residents with private wells should continue annual testing because private wells are not covered by the county system.
